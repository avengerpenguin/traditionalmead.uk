Dragonsbreath: Aegir

Named after Ægir, a sea giant from Norse mythology, this mead from The Lancashire Mead Company is a Melomel (Fruit-flavoured Mead) flavoured with orange juice and English Breakfast tea.

Appearance

The colour is golden with slight hints of orange. The alcohol streaks impressively on the glass, which isn’t surprising with its 17% ABV strength.
